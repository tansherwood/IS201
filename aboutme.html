About Me

Hello! My name is Tanner Sherwood. I am a college student who wants to learn more about business! I have a goal to graduate with a Master's Degree in Accounting. I have enjoyed my time and BYU so far and am looking forward to the rest of my time here! I have many hobbies, including tennis, pickleball, music, and the cello.

Some of my biggest hobbies are outdoor activities. I love hiking, camping, and boating. It has been so cool being in Utah because there are so many cool hikes to go on! I have done a couple in Provo, including hiking the Y. I'm looking forward to exploring more of Utah's amazing landscape in the future!

One of the best experiences of my life was not related to one of the hobbies I mentioned. It was when I went on my mission in California! It was so amazing and I met so many awesome people there. I think it is important to be active in life and try new things. Find things that you enjoy!
