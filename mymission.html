My Mission

I had the awesome opportunity to serve as a full-time missionary is Sacramento, California for two years. I got to work with other missionaries to support the members of the Church there and help others come unto Christ by helping them learn about His teachings.

I served in multiple areas around Sacramento, including, Folsom, El Dorado County, and Calaveras County. I grew to love that people that I served in these places and I also came to know better for myself that God loves all of His children everywhere.
